About
A driven Software Developer, passionate and confident in applying my creativity and skills in building ideas to reality, and profering solutions.

I am Golden Dennar Tanimowo, a Frontend Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria. As an individual, I have always been intrigued about the concept of creating and bringing new ideas to fruition. This brought about my decision in transitioning my career to software engineering.

I have 2 years experience as a software developer with stong focus on React.js, Javascript, HTML, CSS in building single page and web applications. Prior to this time, I have over 4 years experience spanned across Customer experience, Operations and Project management.

Currently, I enjoy and I am interested in front-end development roles. I am excited to make an impact and contribute to meaningful and exciting projects.
